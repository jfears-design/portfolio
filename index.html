Perspective

Every industry offers a new perspective. Every product reveals another pattern. The best ideas emerge when those insights come together.

The most valuable lessons I've learned aren't tied to a single industry. They're rooted in the patterns that run through them: how people build trust, make decisions, navigate complexity, and accomplish their goals.

Those patterns have become the foundation of how I approach every new challenge.

I've always been drawn to unfamiliar problems. Whether I'm designing enterprise software, connected device experiences, consumer products, or AI experiences, I enjoy learning the domain, understanding the people behind it, and uncovering the opportunities others might miss.
